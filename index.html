Hi, I'm Kylie! I'm a full-time student at Western Washington University in the beautiful city of Bellingham, studying Behavioral Neuroscience with an emphasis in Sociology.

Ever since I was little, I've had a passion for all things creative. When I first recieved a DSLR camera for my birthday, I fell in love with its potential to capture moments with vibrant and beautiful detail, and I started taking photos of everything from my friends and family, to tiny ice crystals clinging to tree branches. I feel like photography alllows people to see the beauty in the mundane, capturing something wonderful in the things we see on a typical day. I edit all of my photos with dedication and an eye for detail until they suit both my standards and those of the poeple I'm taking them for.
